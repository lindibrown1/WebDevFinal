Facts and Figures

As of right now, Park City is the largest ski resort in the country, followed by Big Sky, Montana as number 2.


For the highest elevation, we actually have solitude in first, and nordic in last. When night skiing at powder mountian, you can actually see the entire Nordic Valley resort, previously called Wolf Mountian, farther down the mountian.


Favorite Lifts and Runs

Just some of my personal opinitons about the lifts at Snowbasin.

Needles Goldola: Seats 6 people, and is arguably the main lift at Basin. Lines? Terrible. Avoid it at all costs, unless your're taking it to another lift.

Strawberry Gondola: Seats 6 people. . Great lift, almost never crowded. Contains some of the best powder terrain on the mountian. Unfortunatly, it is often closed due to high winds. Also, the lodge there is very small.

John Paul Lift: Seats 4 people, and unlike the first two gondolas, this is a lift so you are open to the world, but you get to keep your skis on. The John Paul lodge also has the best waffles I've ever eaten. Good lodge, lift is not often crowded, and pretty good skiing that connects to the main trails.
