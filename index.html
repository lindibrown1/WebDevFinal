This year I'm Skiing Park City and Snowbasin with the Epic Local Pass. After switching from resort to resort I decided to compile a list of the resorts I've actually been to in Utah, and compare each.

The Resorts In This Analasys Include:

  1. Solitude , where I learned to ski

  2. Snowbasin, where I've skied the most

  3. Powder Mountian, where I skied for a season

  4. Nordic Valley, where I've gone night skiing

  5. Park City, where I have a pass this year
